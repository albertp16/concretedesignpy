Column Biaxial P-M-M Interaction

3D Interaction Surface — Fiber Approach — NSCP 2015 / ACI 318-19

Theory & References \u25BC

The biaxial interaction surface is generated by rotating the neutral axis from 0° to 180° and sweeping its depth at each angle. The section is discretized into a 2D fiber grid. Whitney stress block (0.85f'c over depth a = β1c) is applied to fibers on the compression side of the inclined NA.

\[ P_n = \sum_{i} \sigma_i \cdot A_i \quad;\quad M_{nx} = \sum_{i} \sigma_i \cdot A_i \cdot y_i \quad;\quad M_{ny} = \sum_{i} \sigma_i \cdot A_i \cdot x_i \]

Anwar & Najam (2017), Ch. 3, Eqs. 3.23 — Capacity Interaction Surface

References: NSCP 2015 / ACI 318-19, Anwar & Najam (2017) Ch. 3 & 5
